Cadeaux Show Stables

Cadeaux Show Stables is the premier show stable of Charlottesville, Virginia. Operated by Zach Parks, Cadeaux has a proven track record of success.

About The Name

Cadeaux was the 2007 National Champion Small Junior Hunter ridden by Zach. More importantly though, "cadeaux" is French for "gift". As we reflect back on the events, we are humbled and touched by the outpouring of generosity and support that has been shown to us by the horse community, our clients, our families, and our friends.
